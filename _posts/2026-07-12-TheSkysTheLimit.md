# Important takeaways:
1. Love yourself - have the highest standards for yourself
2. Watch out for authoritarian thinking! Learn to break dichotomous thinking.
3. The human need to do meaningful work (not the same as purpose!)
4. Winning all the time - there's no such thing as a loser
5. Learn to set no limits on your growth
